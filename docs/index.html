Privacy Policy

App name: 1st Dog Whistle
Last updated: March 20, 2026

Overview

1st Dog Whistle is a simple, offline audio tone generator designed to help with dog training. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The app does not:

  • Collect your name, email address, or any identifying information
  • Access your location, camera, microphone, contacts, or files
  • Send any data to external servers or third parties
  • Use analytics, advertising, or tracking services
  • Require any special device permissions

Local Storage

The app saves your last selected frequency locally on your device using Android's SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device and is only used to restore your preferred frequency when you reopen the app.

Children's Privacy

Because the app collects no data whatsoever, it is safe for all ages, including children under 13. No personal information is gathered from any user.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect data.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
